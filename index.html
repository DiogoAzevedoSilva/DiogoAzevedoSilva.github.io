About me

I spent 12 years as a musical analysis teacher and composer at Esproarte in Mirandela, Portugal, where I taught students to recognise patterns, decompose complex structures, and communicate their findings clearly. It turns out those are exactly the skills that matter most in data analytics.

In 2025 I completed an intensive full-time Data Analytics bootcamp at Le Wagon, where I worked end-to-end with real-world datasets — from raw data extraction and cleaning through to machine learning models and interactive dashboards. The bootcamp confirmed what I already suspected: analytical thinking transfers directly, and I genuinely enjoy this work.

I'm currently building my portfolio and looking for my first data analyst role. I'm particularly drawn to positions where clear communication of insights matters as much as the technical work — bridging the gap between data and decision-makers is where I feel most at home.
